## Ingredients

- 500 g flour
- 250 ml warm milk
- 80 g sugar
- 2 eggs
- 50 g butter
- 25 g fresh yeast

## Preparation

1. Mix yeast with milk and sugar.
2. Add flour, eggs, melted butter and knead into a smooth dough.
3. Let rise for 1 hour.
4. Bake at 180Â°C for 35 minutes with crumble on top.
